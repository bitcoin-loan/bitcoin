How Bitcoin Loan Platform Works

1️⃣ Register & Verify Email

Create an account by entering your full name, email, country, password, and your Bitcoin wallet address. Verify your email using the 6-digit code sent to you.

2️⃣ Login to Dashboard

Enter your registered email and password to access your personalized dashboard.

3️⃣ Check BTC Price & Maximum Loan

The dashboard shows the current Bitcoin price and your maximum borrow limit.

4️⃣ Calculate Loan

Enter the amount of BTC you want to borrow. The system calculates the USD value and required 5% collateral automatically.

5️⃣ Deposit Collateral & Request Loan

Click "Request Loan & Deposit Collateral". The collateral is sent to the platform wallet, and your loan is approved automatically.

6️⃣ Loan Disbursement

Once approved, your BTC loan is sent directly to your Bitcoin wallet. You can track all your loans in the "Loan History" section.

7️⃣ Profile & Loan History

View your profile, wallet, verified badge, and all previous loans in one place. Copy your wallet address easily with a click.
